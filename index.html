03 · Method

A measurement system, not a marketing package.

Every engagement runs on PRISM™, our five-phase framework:

  1. Profile

    We map exactly where you appear — and don't — across all 7 platforms, query by query.

  2. Reverse

    We reverse-engineer why the AI recommends who it recommends in your segment.

  3. Intervene

    We fix the structural gaps: how your expertise, credentials, and specialties are described in the sources AI reads.

  4. Signal

    We build the citations and third-party presence AI platforms treat as ground truth.

  5. Monitor

    We track your visibility monthly, because AI answers shift.

You get an AVI™ score (0–100) across four dimensions — Presence, Position, Sentiment, Context — so progress is measured, not claimed.

Built on a finding: top producers are invisible in AI.

ASTONTO was built on a finding: the top-producing real estate agents are often invisible in AI search. Not because they lack credibility — but because AI doesn't see closings.
